Welcome to the battlefield! Time to take on enemy!

Move troops units to make powerful walls and attack formations. You can move a troop by tapping and dragging. You can remove a troop by double tapping. 3 troops in a horizontal row of the same color make a wall:

3 troops of the same color vertically make an attack formation:

Creatures can be prepared to attack by putting troop units of the same color behind them, smaller creatures require two troops directly behind them in a column, gigantic creatures require four units behind them in a square. The number of turns remaining before attack formations launched is indicated in the green charging orb.


Troops and creatures can be removed from the battlefield by tapping on them twice. Don't forget to call your troops to the battlefield when your side is looking kind of empty.

Syncing your units gives you an extra power boost! You can sync your units by preparing them to attack in the same turn.
